Bio

Meet Cute formed in Sacramento, CA in 2016 with Jacob Waite and Taylor Kohl. Having a revolving door of line up changes for touring, they finally settled on the current lineup with Gabe Merriman and Austin Whitted. No specific sound direction or genre depiction, Meet Cute has been known for its primordial drums style and feedback heavy guitar sounds. They recently recorded the debut LP with Steve Albini at his studio, Electrical Audio in Chicago, IL
